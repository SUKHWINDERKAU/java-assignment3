Did You Know…?


One of the most important components of effective life-long learning is play. Learning a new skill (like BLACK MAGIC)should include a certain amount of play. Renowned children’s programming entertainer Mr. Rogers certainly knew about the value that play represented:


>

Play is often talked about as if it were a relief from serious learning. But for children, play is serious learning. Play is really the work of childhood. –Fred Rogers



Take a little time every day to just play with technology – you’ll be surprised how much you learn.
